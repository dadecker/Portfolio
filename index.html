Spring 2018 Senior Project

USF In Cooperation With Raytheon

About

Since 2015, the Federal Aviation Authority (FAA) has allowed hobbyist the permission to fly personal drones (less than 55lbs) under very strict conditions

Although some of these restrictions sound simple to adhere to, the average drone pilot is unaware of the restricted areas in their vicinity imposed by the FAA.

The goal of this project is to develop a secure application that shows a users current position and velocity in relation other objects in its vicinity.

The end state of the project is to be able to display live tracks (feeds) of commercial airlines operating in the vicinity (in all three dimensions) of the users drone as well as other cooperative friendly drones operating nearby.

Objective

Develop a system that allows a drone pilot to determine how close they are operating to a restricted area in real time.
